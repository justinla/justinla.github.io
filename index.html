Welcome!

Air quality has become an increasingly large issue in todays rural and urban areas. This poses a particularly hazardous issue for people who are already suffering from respiritory issues. We are working to develop an effortless system for Asthma patients to keep themsleves, their physicians, and their family up to date with their Peak Flow information.

About

Peak Flow Monitor is a simple solution to help Asthma patients track their Peak Flow throughout the day that also allows them to share their information easily with their family and physicians.



We are a technology company based in Buffalo NY.
